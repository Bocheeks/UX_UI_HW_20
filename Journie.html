Journie

We worked on Journie to introduce an efficient way of tracking, performing, and sharing your nutritional and workout routine.

It first began with a team of 3 who were all passionate and have a deep understanding of working out. Even though each of us know a lot about working out we still set out to research what others would want in such application. Our research discovered that the current application whom also exist for the same purpose does not have the capability to share. With this insight we set out to create Journie with Social Media capabilities
